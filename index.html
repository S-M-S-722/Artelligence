About Us

Who we are

Artelligence is a student-led organization (est. 2021) based in Kerala, South India. Our main focus is to create meaningful learning experiences, host cultural and educational events, and support youth with skills in arts, IT, and public speaking.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to inspire creativity, knowledge, and teamwork among students. We envision a community where every young person has the skills and confidence to lead projects, express themselves, and build a brighter future.

What we do

  • Organise workshops: art, public speaking, coding
  • Run events & competitions: drawing, essays, music
  • Publish articles and guides through our Publishing Division
  • Mentoring and skill-based training for students
