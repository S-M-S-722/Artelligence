About Artelligence 2025

Artelligence is the premier art festival of 2025, bringing together talented students from various institutions to showcase their creativity, intelligence, and artistic abilities. Our festival celebrates the intersection of art and intelligence, highlighting how creative expression and intellectual pursuits complement each other.

With competitions spanning across four major categories - Creatia, Log, General, and Vocalizia - Artelligence provides a platform for students to demonstrate their skills in visual arts, writing, general knowledge, and performing arts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration among students through competitive yet friendly artistic and intellectual challenges. We believe that nurturing both artistic expression and intellectual curiosity creates well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

Event Categories

Creatia: Visual arts including drawing, painting, calligraphy, and digital art

Log: Literary arts including essay writing, poetry, and storytelling in multiple languages

General: Intellectual challenges including quizzes, spelling bees, and problem-solving

Vocalizia: Performing arts including singing, speech, and dramatic performances
